# Clay · כלי

Israeli pottery brand. Traditional + contemporary ceramics for local market.

## Rules

- Always reference `design-tokens.css` for colors, type, and spacing — never hardcode values
- Logo counter fill must match background color
- Glaze blue (`--clay-glaze`) only for interactive elements, never decoration
- Light mode only — cream backgrounds, warm neutrals

## Design Context

### Users
Local Israelis looking for quality handmade homewares. They encounter Clay at Friday markets, fairs, and online. Browsing, not on a mission. They value authenticity over polish.

### Brand Personality
**Warm · Sincere · Down-to-earth** — the neighborhood potter.

### Aesthetic Direction
Earthy & tactile — texture-forward, material-honest. Terracotta on warm cream. Cormorant + DM Sans. No dark mode.

### Design Principles
1. **Material honesty** — surfaces feel physical (parchment, fired clay)
2. **Earned color** — glaze blue only when its absence would be a loss
3. **Imperfection as intent** — organic irregularity, not machine-smooth
4. **Quiet confidence** — large type, generous margins, rewards a second look
5. **Workshop over gallery** — process over pedestal, studio over museum
