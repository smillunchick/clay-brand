# Clay — Brand Guide

## Brand Essence

**Sacred in the everyday.**

Clay is an Israeli pottery studio making traditional and contemporary ceramics for locals who value quality handmade homewares. Sold at markets, fairs, and online.

The name is a bilingual pun: "Clay" (the material) / "כלי" (kli — vessel) / "כלי קודש" (klei kodesh — holy vessels). Everyday objects with sacred resonance.

---

## Personality

**Warm · Sincere · Down-to-earth**

The neighborhood potter. Approachable but never casual about the craft. Every piece carries the warmth of the hands that shaped it.

---

## Logo

The wordmark is "Clay" rendered in Hebrew-influenced calligraphic letterforms. The angular strokes and pen-angle modulation reference Hebrew script tradition — the bilingual meaning lives in the visual treatment, not in a separate Hebrew wordmark.

**Signature details:**
- Dot inside the C counter
- Two dots below the a/y baseline
- Organic stroke variation (intentional, not sloppy)

**Files:**
- `assets/clay-logo.svg` — primary logo (transparent background)
- `assets/clay-logo-vectorized.svg` — Recraft vectorization (with background)

**Usage rules:**
- Primary use: terracotta on cream
- Reversed: cream on terracotta or dark
- Minimum size: 32px width (dots become illegible below this)
- The 'a' counter fill must match the background color

## Color

### Primary — Terracotta
The color of sun-dried clay. Warm, grounded, material.

| Role | Hex | Physical descriptor |
|------|-----|-------------------|
| Primary | `#A25D39` | Sun-dried terracotta |
| Dark | `#813F25` | Fired stoneware |
| Light | `#B6704E` | Desert sandstone |
| Muted | `#C4926F` | Weathered clay |

### Background — Cream
| Role | Hex | Physical descriptor |
|------|-----|-------------------|
| Cream | `#F2EFE4` | Kiln-fired parchment |
| Warm | `#EDE8D8` | Unglazed bisque |
| Cool | `#F5F3EC` | Limestone dust |

### Secondary — Glaze Blue
Drawn from traditional Israeli ceramic glazes. Cool counterpoint to warm terracotta. Appears only for actions and interactive elements, never decoration.

| Role | Hex |
|------|-----|
| Glaze | `#4A6B7A` |
| Light | `#6B8D9C` |
| Muted | `#8EAAB5` |

### Neutral Scale
Warm earth tones from near-white to near-black: `#FAF9F5` (50) through `#1E1B17` (900).

Full values in `design-tokens.css`.

## Typography

### Display — Cormorant
Garamond descendant. Elegant high-contrast letterforms that bridge the calligraphic logo and readable body text. Used for headlines and feature text.

- **Light (300):** Hero headlines, large display
- **Regular (400):** Section headings
- **Italic (300):** Accent text, Hebrew references

### Body — DM Sans
Warm geometric sans. Clean without being clinical. Stays out of the way.

- **Regular (400):** Body copy
- **Light (300):** Secondary text, captions
- **Medium (500):** Labels, navigation, uppercase meta

### Scale
Perfect Fourth ratio (1.333). See `design-tokens.css` for exact values.

## Voice

### Attributes
- **Warm** — not cute, not folksy. The warmth of handmade, not Hallmark.
- **Direct** — says what it means. No marketing language.
- **Grounded** — speaks from the workshop, not the gallery.

### This, not that
| This | Not that |
|------|----------|
| "Handmade in Israel" | "Artisanally crafted ceramic experiences" |
| "For your table" | "Elevate your dining aesthetic" |
| "Each piece is different" | "Every creation is a unique masterpiece" |
| "Come find us at the market" | "Visit our exclusive pop-up showcase" |

### Tone modulation
- **Market stall:** Friendly, inviting, casual
- **Online shop:** Clear, descriptive, matter-of-fact
- **Social media:** Personal, behind-the-scenes, process-focused
- **Packaging:** Minimal. Name, origin, care instructions.

## Application Notes

### What never appears
- Gradients
- Drop shadows
- Neon or saturated accent colors
- Stock photography
- Machine-made uniformity
- The word "luxury" or "premium"

### Photography direction
- Natural light, warm tones
- Real hands, real clay, real kitchens
- Close-up textures, shallow depth of field
- Process shots (wheel, kiln, glazing)
- No staged perfection — beauty in the imperfect

### Print
- CMYK equivalents needed for market signage and business cards
- Primary terracotta CMYK: approximately C:0 M:52 Y:72 K:30
- Use uncoated stock to reinforce the material, tactile brand feel
